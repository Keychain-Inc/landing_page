Scry

Scry is an open-source, permissionless, and flexible framework for independent Oracle deployment. It offers high-scale batching, revenue earning, cross-chain data, and instant deployment. With Scry, anyone can run their own fully independent, autonomous oracle without coding, deployable in less than 5 minutes.

Discover Scry

addrs

addrs simplifies cryptocurrency transactions by allowing users to create payment profiles containing all their addresses in one place. Compatible with various wallets and protocols, addrs is a non-custodial platform that gives users full control over their assets. It also enables receiving crypto using Visa, Mastercard, or bank accounts without the need for a custodial account.

Simplify with addrs

Vain

Vain is an open marketplace for securely mining vanity ETH addresses using ECC offsets of public keys. It provides a unique and personalized way to obtain custom Ethereum addresses that stand out from the crowd.

Mine Your Vanity Address

Kaku

Kaku is a decentralized platform for commissioning art. Artists can showcase their work, and users can request commissions with attached bounties. The commissioned art is minted as an NFT, ensuring provenance and allowing the owner to show it off. Kaku provides a seamless and transparent way to support artists and acquire unique digital art pieces.

Commission Art on Kaku

Signet

Signet is a platform that allows creators to sign NFTs for their fans and collectors to gather signatures from their favorite creators. It provides a unique way to showcase and authenticate digital autographs on the blockchain. Users can display their signed NFTs, known as Signets, and engage with their favorite creators in a new and exciting way.

Get Your Signet

Conjure

Conjure is a permissionless platform that allows anyone to create tokenized assets on Ethereum, representing any imaginable asset. The resulting Synths are fully collateralized by Ether and have their value pegged via a flexible combination of multiple arbitrary price sources. Conjure enables the creation of synthetic currencies, tokens from other blockchains, real-world commodities, and exotic assets like basket currencies and indices.

Conjure Your Assets

the Wall

The Wall is a decentralized messaging platform that allows users to post permanent, on-chain messages called tags. Each message is wrapped as an NFT, making it fully ownable. Users earn 10 $BRK tokens per message sent, incentivizing participation. The Wall provides a unique way to communicate and leave a lasting mark on the blockchain.

Tag the Wall

alchemy

Alchemy allows you to create a tokenized DAO for any NFT collection. Transform your NFTs into liquid, community-controlled assets that can be traded, voted on, and used to manage your NFTs. With Alchemy, you can create funds, communities, and split collections between friends, all without coding or technical experience. Unlock the full potential of your NFTs with Alchemy.
